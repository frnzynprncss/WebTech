Present is Present

Director: Huang Yuanda

Casts: Fan Zhi Xin, Tu Bing

Wei Zi Qi, the carefree heir of a wealthy family, finds himself in a fierce power struggle to gain control of his father’s firm. His antics often test the patience of his diligent executive assistant, Yuan Xia. However, when Yuan Xia is falsely accused of misconduct, she is forced out of the firm. Her troubles escalate as she collapses, ends up in hospital, and is then diagnosed with a terminal disease. At her lowest point, she receives a phone call from the “future” Zi Qi. Could this be a sign that the “present-day” Zi Qi could save her from disaster? And could love follow as the intrigue deepens?

9.0/10 Rating Score
