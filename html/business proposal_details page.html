Business Proposal

Director: Son Jung Hyun

Casts: Ahn Hyo Seop, Kim Se Jeong, Seol In Ah, Kim Min Kyu

Business Proposal follows the story of a young woman who reluctantly agrees to go on a blind date on behalf of her friend, only to discover that her date is the CEO of her company. Romantic mishaps ensue as the two try to keep their relationship a secret.
